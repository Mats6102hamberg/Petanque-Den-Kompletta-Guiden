PÉTANQUE

From Beginner to Pro – A Complete Guide

Foreword

Pétanque is more than just a sport – it's a lifestyle, a social activity, and both a tactical and technical challenge. This book is written to give beginners a solid foundation and offer experienced players new perspectives and improvement tips. Whether you're holding a boule for the first time or already competing at a high level, there's something here for you.

    What is Boule Pétanque?

  • Chapter 2
    Equipment – Choose the Right Boules
    Weight, size and material, difference between competition and recreational boules, maintenance and cleaning
  • Chapter 3
    Basic Techniques
    Proper grip and stance, how to point, how to shoot, training with measurable goals
  • Chapter 4
    Strategy for Beginners
    Simple tactics to win points, decision tree (point or shoot?), how to read the terrain, common mistakes
